What We Offer

As always, partners grows with you!

Planning

Our team of expert engineers design mining plans for both coal and non - coal mines including environmental management plans (EMP). Optimizing the exploitation of mineral reserves for maximum added value.

Resource Estimation:

Estimating mineral resources is performed at all stages of the mine life cycle. We will ensure you get the maximum value out of the available information. investigate data quality, focus on the reliable data, discount unreliable, erroneous data and produce accurate resource estimates.

Cost Estimation

Financial feasibility of projects with estimation on capex and opex required for a mining project.

Equipment

We provide assistance in selection, supply, erection, and installation of equipment in mines and coal handling plants.

Infrastructure

We handle the design and execution of works relating to the constructions of drains and road diversions in the mine areas.

Scheduling

We offer mining timelines using state of the art technologies, with a daily target of production and monitoring of the timelines periodically.

Performance Evaluation

We offer constant monitoring of performance of the mine, and make value propositions to increase productivity.

Safety Auditing

We conduct timely safety audits, and provide qualitative solutions for safety upgradation.

Documentation

We provide consulting solutions for evaluating technical and financial bid documents for mining projects.

Research and Studies

Our team of esteemed engineers offer varied studies involving exploration, surface subsidence, scientific studies, 2d seismic studies, EIA/EMP, EC, FC, Mining Plan approval etc.

Modelling

We offer numerical and 3D modelling services giving out mine structures and reserve estimates.
