Geospark is a team of immensely experienced and self-motivated engineers that have come together to address the needs of mining industry. With a vast experience across a broad spectrum of mining industries both coal and non-coal, our team has hands on experience in identification of coal blocks, mine planning, designing, scheduling, and detailed project reporting.

Our execution team extends un-paralleled support in successful execution of under-ground and open cast mining, equipment selection and supply, budgeting, and operational management. Our services include planning for shaft sinking, tunneling, pit/hill slope analysis, stabilization of slopes, rock bolting, and roof roof/sides reinforcement.

We offer professional guidance for bid preparation and mining bid estimations. Our company’s genesis can be attributed to the acute need of a consulting service by providing end to end solutions for the mining industry.
